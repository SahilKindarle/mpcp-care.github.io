AMLA BENEFITS

HEALTHY BENEFITS :-

  1. Reduce dry skin.
  2. Improve brain health and function.
  3. Reduce premature pigment loss from hair, or greying .
  4. Reduce the appearance of scars, blemishes, age spots, and wrinkles.

TIPS-

Silk Advance Amla oil is often used in place of a conditioner after shampooing. It is gently massaged into the scalp so that it lightly coats the hair. People with an oily scalp should apply it to the hair shafts only. After 15 minutes or so, thoroughly rinse the hair with warm water. Silk Advance Amla oil can also be left on the scalp overnight for deep conditioning. If used in this way, wear a shower cap and cover your pillows with towels to prevent staining. Amla oil can have a strong, musky odor, so you may want to use it sparingly if applied overnight.

    HEENA BENEFITS

    HEALTHY BENEFITS :-

    1. Nourishes the scalp.
    2. Restores the pH value of the scalp.
    3. Stimulates healthy hair growth.
    4. Prevent premature graying of hair.

    TIPS-

    Ingredients:

    1. Fresh henna leaves
    2. 500 ml. of coconut oil

    Method:
    1. Grind fresh henna leaves into a smooth paste of thick consistency just like we used to prepare the henna paste before the advent of the Mehendi cones. Don’t use too much water for this. Adding only a few drops will do.
    2. Once the paste cools down, make several small gooseberry-sized balls out of it.
    3. Let the fresh henna paste balls dry for a while. Meanwhile, heat the coconut oil in a pan.
    4. Gently put the henna balls into the coconut oil and allow them to boil together.
    5. Remove the pan when the color of the oil changes into a dark brown to brown shade.
    6. Let the oil cool down and strain it. You can even let the boiled mixture sit overnight and strain it the next day.
    7. Store the strained oil in an airtight container. Your henna hair oil for hair growth is ready!
